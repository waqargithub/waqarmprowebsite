Width Measurement

Width Measurement  Height Measurement  
  1. Measure width in inches at top, middle, and center of window opening to get three width measurements as shown in image: Width A, Width B, and Width C.
  2. Round down to the near 1/8 in. Thus, if measurement is 34 13/16", round down to 34 3/4.
  3. Take the smallest of Width A, Width B, and Width C. Treat that as your measured width.
  4. If mounting outside the window on the wall, add 4 inches to measured width, and treat the result as measured width.

Height Measurement

Width Measurement  Height Measurement  
  1. Measure height in inches at top, middle, and center of window opening to get three width measurements as shown in image: Height A, Height B, and Height C.
  2. Round up to the near 1/8 in. Thus, if measurement is 34 13/16", round up to 34 7/8.
  3. Take the largest of Height A, Height B, and Height C. Treat that as your measured height.
  4. If mounting outside the window on the wall, add 4 inches to measured height, and treat the result as measured height.
