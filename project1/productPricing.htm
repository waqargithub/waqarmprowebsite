Blinds Price Charts

To price your blind:

  1. Go to the appropriate price grid based on the material you are considering
  2. Find the first column that has a width greater than or equal to that of your blind
  3. Go down the column to the first row that has a height greater than or equal to that of your blind
  4. Note the price

For example, a wood blind of width 34 and height 51 would be priced by going to the width column of 36, and going down this column to the height row of 60, which gives the price of $124.95.
